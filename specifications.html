We certainly help our clients in the approval process through the professionals in our company. However, we are not directly involved in the approval process but our employees ensure that the clients get a completely hassle free experience by assisting them in the process.

We have our own set of quality parameters that it abides by to maintain optimum quality of the work we execute. We also do a thorough background check of our suppliers and contractors to ensure they follow the company's quality standards.

We follow a milestone based payment methodology, where you will pay as per the agreed payment milestones in the construction services agreement which is agreed upon before the work has started.

It is not necessary for all houses to have a compound wall. By removing the compound wall from the package, we ensure that only the clients who opt for a compound wall, pay for it.

The pricing of our contracts is frozen on a stamp paper. Thus, the prices will remain stable in most of the scenarios. However, in rare situations where there are sudden spikes in prices due to external scenarios, the price variations are borne by the client. On the other hand, if there is a price drop, the client gets a price reduction for the same.
